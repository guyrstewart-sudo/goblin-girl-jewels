The Maker

Hands that spin fire, solder crystals.

Goblin Girl Jewels is the craft of Larissa Alboucrek — a flow artist and fire dancer who turns sustainably harvested earth materials into wearable magick.

The same warm, wide-open festival energy that moves a hoop of flame goes into every piece: raw stones gathered with reverence, then hand-soldered and wire-wrapped one at a time. Nothing is mass-made. Every talisman is set with love and intention, built for spiritual and earthside protection — meant to be danced in, lived in, and grown old with.

No two pieces are alike, because no two stones — or the people who wear them — ever are.

Materials & method

Sustainably harvested

Crystals and stones sourced with respect for the land — small-batch, never strip-mined bulk.

Soldered & wrapped

Hand-soldered bezels, wire wraps, and electroformed copper — old-craft techniques, one piece at a time.

Intentioned

Each talisman is cleansed and set with intention before it leaves the studio.

Crystal care

Keep your magick charged

Keep pieces dry and off in the shower or ocean. Cleanse stones under moonlight or with sage / palo santo. Copper will patina beautifully over time — polish gently with a soft cloth to brighten.
